I am pi-dal, a student from Guangdong moving between code, mathematics, physics, and ordinary life.

This site is my public notebook. I keep notes, experiments, and pieces of life here when they feel worth returning to. It is not meant to be exhaustive, only honest.

I mainly build with Python, Vue, React, and CSS. Outside programming, I spend time on basketball, table tennis, badminton, guitar, astronomy, and the side interests that start from curiosity and slowly turn into their own paths.

`pi-dal` comes from Raspberry Pi, Python, π, and my English name, David Albt Leonardo. It is a point of departure, not a boundary.
