summary: >-
  Hermann Hesse has always maintained a close relationship with nature. He drew
  inspiration from the four seasons and wrote a large number of exquisitely
  conceived poems and prose. Ulrike Anders has carefully selected and compiled
  these wonderful chapters to show Hesse's unique insight into the unique magic
  of the four seasons: the eagerness of spring to revive everything, the
  exuberance and beauty of summer scenery, the brilliant changes of autumn
  colors, and the contemplative tranquility of winter snowscapes. Hesse was
  completely absorbed in observing and describing the nature and its changes
  around him. His beautiful words accompany us through every season, year after
  year.
