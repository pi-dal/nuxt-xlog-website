  Steve Jobs' roller-coaster life and fiery personality made him a legend. He
  was a highly creative business leader. His pursuit of perfection and
  unyielding passion led to disruptive changes in six major industries,
  including personal computers, animated films, music, mobile phones, tablets
  and digital publishing. In 2011, "The Biography of Steve Jobs" was published;
  today, two years later, we launched a revised edition. On the basis of the
  original "Jobs Biography", the entire translation has been revised, and many
  suggestions from readers on the translation have been adopted, making the
  overall translation more complete and sophisticated. In addition, a highlight
  of the revised edition is the addition of a postscript written by the author
  himself, describing the relevant processes after Steve Jobs's death, including
  the funeral, etc., which has new attractions for readers and makes this
  classic book more perfect.
