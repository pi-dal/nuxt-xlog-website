The day after our provincial exam ended on July 8, our physics camp departed from the high-speed rail station. After 8 hours, we arrived in Hangzhou, beginning an 18-day journey.

### Departure

Around 3 PM on July 9, we boarded the high-speed train for an 8-hour ride (including a 50-minute transfer). The scenery was beautiful along the way, and listening to podcasts and reading e-books made the time fly by.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_0011.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;Departure&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 2)" title="Image from Departure" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

Finally, around 11 PM, we arrived in Hangzhou and took the training center's shuttle to [Hangzhou Normal University's Cangqian Campus](https://www.hznu.edu.cn/c/2016-03-16/768080.shtml) (HNU), arriving around 11:50 PM.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_0022.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;Departure&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 3)" title="Image from Departure" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

## Life at HNU

Time at HNU was a mix of intense study and laid-back moments — truly a wonderful experience.

### Teaching

Three professors taught the program (Cui Hongbin, Lv Junli, Ye Bangjiao). Their teaching was excellent, and I gained a lot of new knowledge and techniques. The assistants' exam papers and explanations were... eye-opening. Although I got crushed by the top students in several exams, I managed to hold my own and learned a lot.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_1372.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;Teaching&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 4)" title="Image from Teaching" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

### Scenery

As HNU's main campus, Cangqian is known for its beautiful environment. Tree-lined paths and lush gardens make it one of Hangzhou's most beautiful campuses.

### Food & Accommodation

We stayed in Building 6, Shuyuan. For 90 yuan a day, the conditions were acceptable. Meals were mostly at Cafeteria 3. The campus also had a supermarket, Mixue Ice Cream, and KFC.

The most convenient transportation was biking. I highly recommend it — just don't go too fast.

### Activities

Although the schedule was packed (9 hours of class daily), we found time to relax. Hax convinced us to get bubble tea almost every two days. The campus library was excellent, especially the math and physics section.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_0523.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;Activities&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 7)" title="Image from Activities" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

### Highlights

Every day at HNU was joyful. Teacher Mu brought us watermelon, Teacher Zhao bought us bubble tea, and everyone got along well. Hax even helped me overcome my fear of talking to girls. The most absurd thing was that I went to the clinic twice — once when Hax got heatstroke, and once when Hax injured her foot.

## West Lake Trip

On the afternoon of the 25th, our training ended and we took a bus to West Lake.

We found a Hangzhou restaurant nearby (Hangzhou really is a food desert). Then we visited Wulin Night Market (too crowded for this introvert). We walked for over two hours around West Lake, racking up nearly 20,000 steps. The night scenery was beautiful, though my photography skills don't do it justice.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_1127.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;West Lake Trip&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 8)" title="Image from West Lake Trip" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

The next day, we visited Leifeng Pagoda, helping an elderly man buy his ticket along the way. We climbed to the top and enjoyed the magnificent view of West Lake.

<img src="/article-assets/2023-Hangzhou-Travelling/IMG_1208.jpeg" alt="Illustration from &quot;West Lake Trip&quot; in 2023 Physics Camp in Hangzhou (image 9)" title="Image from West Lake Trip" loading="lazy" decoding="async">

## Return

After lunch, we took an hour-long subway to Xiaoshan International Airport and flew out of Hangzhou at 6 PM. The sunset from the plane was absolutely beautiful!
