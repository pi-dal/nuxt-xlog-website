summary: >-
  This is not a story about success, but a story about growth. This book will
  completely change the way we think about Steve Jobs. Steve Jobs is one of the
  most famous CEOs in history. There are many books written about him, but this
  book is different. This book overturns myths and clichés about Steve Jobs,
  including that he was a combination of genius and jerk, irritable, selfish,
  and neglectful of his friends and family. This book reveals the family life
  and career of the Apple co-founder and CEO, and answers a key question: How
  did a young man so arrogant and arrogant that he was driven out of Apple
  become the most effective and visionary business leader in history, changing
  the lives of billions of people? Brent Schlender and Rick Tetzeli use reliable
  sources and even exclusive channels to reconstruct the real Steve, depicting
  how he maximized his strengths and struggled with his shortcomings.
