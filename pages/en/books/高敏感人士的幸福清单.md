summary: >-
  You who always feel that "it's all your fault"; you who are always easily
  moved and afraid of being impulsive; you who are always told that you think
  too much; you who always care about other people's evaluations and are even a
  little glassy. How do you stop yourself from being exhausted every day? What
  if you stop yourself from being so tired? This book is written for those who
  are highly sensitive and have fragile souls. Compared with the people around
  them, they are more susceptible to the influence of their environment and even
  suffer from it; however, they also have the right to pursue happiness. This
  book is divided into 6 chapters to help guide highly sensitive people from
  recognizing their own sensitive emotions, to getting out of their own
  sensitive emotions, to pursuing true self-happiness and correctly handling
  relationships with others. As long as you do a little bit every day, you can
  escape from a highly sensitive life and have a list of happiness for yourself.
